Frequently asked q's

What are the first steps I should take when ordering a wedding cake?

Congratulations on your engagement! We recommend that you contact us at least 4-8 months before your wedding to ensure availability. To begin the process, we’ll schedule a phone consultation to discuss the design details of your wedding. We love to see your Pinterest boards and inspiration images, too, so feel free to send those over prior to our phone call! After we’ve had a chance to discuss your wedding and our process, we’ll schedule a cake tasting and design consultation with Chef Madison.

How do you price your cakes?

Because every cake we create is one of a kind, we do not have standard pricing After an initial design consultation, we will provide you with a custom quote for the cost of your cake. Our minimum order starts at $3000.

Can I order something other than a wedding cake?

Absolutely! We'd love to be a part of your next celebration - we gladly offer birthday celebration cakes, baby shower cakes, bridal party cakes, groom's cakes, corporate event cakes, and Bar/Bat Mitzvah cakes. Please email us for more information!

What is the farthest you deliver? And do you set up the cake for me?

Our delivery fee for orders delivered in New York City have a flat rate of $250. Delivery for anywhere in the world is subject to availability, and a custom quote will be provided based on the delivery location. If an onsite team is needed to set up the cake an onsite fee will be charged.

Are you Kosher?

We are Kosher Certified upon request. If you are looking for a Kosher cake please contact us and we will walk you through our kosher process.

Do you offer internships?

We do! Our interns gain invaluable experience in the kitchen under the direction of Chef Madison. Due to the nature of our kitchen and the limited number of internships we have available, the program is only available for full-time participants completing relevant degree programs. If you are interested in an internship at Madison Lee’s Cakes, please email your cover letter, resume, and portfolio to careers@madisonleescakes.com.

Where are you located?

We are located at 336 W 37th Street Suite 240 in Manhattan (between 8th & 9th). We are a private studio and available by appointment only.

Do you offer classes?

Yes we offer private, group and corporate classes.
